<ul>

<h2>2. Why We Collect Information</h2>
<ul>
<li>Account registration and authentication</li>
<li>Email and mobile verification</li>
<li>Provider verification</li>
<li>Displaying nearby providers</li>
<li>Fraud prevention and security</li>
<li>Subscription management</li>
<li>Customer support</li>
<li>Legal compliance</li>
</ul>

<h2>3. Direct Contact</h2>
<p>Task-Ninja displays a provider's name, service details and contact number. When a user taps the Call button, the application only opens the device's default phone dialer with the provider's number pre-filled. Task-Ninja does not provide in-app calling, chat, messaging, call recording or call monitoring and does not store telephone conversations.</p>

<h2>4. Provider Verification</h2>
<p>Providers must complete identity verification before listing services. Verification may require Aadhaar, PAN, live selfie and address details. Verification is only a review of submitted documents and is not a guarantee of identity, qualifications, conduct or service quality.</p>

<h2>5. Information Sharing</h2>
<p>We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared with trusted infrastructure providers, where required by law, or to investigate fraud and security incidents. Provider contact information is displayed to users for direct service enquiries.</p>

<h2>6. Location</h2>
<p>Location permission is used only to help users discover nearby providers. We do not continuously track users in the background.</p>

<h2>7. Account Deletion</h2>
<p>Users and providers may request account deletion. Deletion may be processed as a soft deletion. Certain records may be retained for fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution and security.</p>

<h2>8. Data Retention</h2>
<p>We may retain verification records, subscription history, security logs, technical logs and deleted account records where permitted or required by law.</p>

<h2>9. Third-Party Services</h2>
<p>Task-Ninja may use Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Cloud Messaging, Google Maps and Google Play Services.</p>

<h2>10. Disclaimer</h2>
<p>Task-Ninja is only a technology platform connecting users with independent service providers. Any communication, agreement, payment or dispute after a user contacts a provider is solely between the user and the provider.</p>
